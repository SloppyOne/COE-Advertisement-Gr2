Welcome to our College of Engineering! Our college typically covers a range of disciplines, including civil engineering , electrical engineering,
mechanical engineering and other related fields. The engineering curriculum at LSPU Sta. is designed to prepare students for successful careers in various
engineering fields. Students enrolled in LSPU Sta. Cruz can expect to engage in both classroom lectures and laboratory work to gain a comprehensive
understanding of engineering principles and practices. The university aims to produce competent engineers who are equipped to address real-world engineering
challenges and contribute to the development of their communities and industries.
